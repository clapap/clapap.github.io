SPARSH

MOTTO AND VISION OF THE GROUP

SENSE OF IMMORTALITY

SPARSH is a cultural society of DCRUST,Murthal founded in 2008 by MR.Dhama with the motto to increase confidence level of the students of the University and to provide a platform to build yourself. Society welfare is another important concern of this group i.e creating awareness amongst the people through the performances about the sensitive topics of the society. With the help of this society students not only become confident but they also find their inner strength and Sparsh also creates a unique bond between the students and this bond is very important for the students staying away from their homes. Sparsh is like a family, we stay together, we learn together and we develop together.
